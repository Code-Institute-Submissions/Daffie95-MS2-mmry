Every 65 seconds, someone in the United States develops Alzheimer's, But while you keep your mind active, your mental health is less likely to decline. In a study where almost 3.000 adults, aged 65 and above took part in brain training sessions. These 3.000 participants showed improvements in memory, reasoning, and speed of processing information for up to 5 years after the study was completed. While dementias is still somewhat uncharted territory, and facts regarding these dementias are uncertain. However, another study shows that exercising the mind might delay rapid decline in thinking skills, such as memory and reasoning.
